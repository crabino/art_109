Ouroboros

My most recent art piece, Ourobos.

Ouroboros is the idea of self destruction from the idea of appeasing those around you to fit into a mold that they want you to fill. This can be seen in interpersonal ideas like relationships or something more in a job focus sense. I wanted to depict the harshness of representing yourself unauthentically, feeling that you are being eaten up inside due to outside forces. Though it is in a sense those forces who watch you fit into the mold you don’t want to, it is you that is consuming yourself.
