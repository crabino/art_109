That was my original goal but as my time during university taught me there are always more ways to tackle a subject. Which is why I would explore more options as an artist to tackle the rigid parts of video and expand my horizon which is why I began to learn for myself the 3d space as a canvas as the tools provided to me can allow me to explore a fantastical way to showcase my views. .
